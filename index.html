I am a dedicated professional in front-end development, imbued with a fervent passion for continuous improvement and technical excellence. With a solid academic background in systems analysis and development from the renowned Faculdade das Américas (FAM), coupled with over a decade of experience as an electronics technician specializing in Apple products at a technical assistance center located in Palmares, Pernambuco, I have crafted a singular and multifaceted trajectory. Furthermore, I maintain an unrelenting pursuit of knowledge, actively engaging in courses on the Alura platform and at DevClub. This rich journey has culminated in the creation and contribution to several notable projects hosted on my GitHub account. Currently based in Ribeirão, I eagerly seek challenging opportunities that allow me to apply my expertise in front-end development, whether in the form of internships or remote work.
